Udyami

Udyami is Panjab University's National Entrepreneurship Summit. Udyami aims to bring together entrepreneurs, students, industrialists and investors to create an ambience for new ideas and startups. Summit will include competitions designed to test and enhance the entrepreneurial skills, workshops and Panel Discussions covering all the major fields of entrepreneurship.
